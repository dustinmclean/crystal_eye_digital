    Let's keep it ridiculously simple!

    It’s nice to start with a short casual chat, to discuss the project, and make sure we’re a solid match to work together. Sometimes chemistry is an important element in getting to the finish line!

    Once we’re on the same page, and have worked together in laying out a strong vision for the project, I’ll get to work on the design process. We can do as many revisions as needed, until you’re stoked on the finished design and layout.

    After you’ve approved the design, I’ll get to work implementing it for the web, hand coding from scratch, and making the site polished, dynamic, and beautifully responsive for any screen size.

    The fun part, signing off on the finished site, choosing the url, and publishing it live. I’ll hand off the metaphorical digital keys, and afterward I’m always happy to stay in touch for any future updates or changes!
