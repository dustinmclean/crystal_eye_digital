    • about

    My name's Dustin McLean, and I’m a web developer, graphic designer, and musician, living on Vancouver Island, Canada.

    I enjoy creating clean, efficient web design and development, and simple, minimalist solutions to design problems. My work often contains hints of psychedelia, vintage design, surrealism, and is inspired by a connection to the natural world. I’m passionate about beautiful, clean, responsive design for the web.

    ‍

    My tech stack currently consists of HTML, CSS, Javascript, version control through Git, with web design done through Procreate, Illustrator and Photoshop. I’m currently learning React, and Node.js, and with previous Wordpress experience, I’m beginning to learn foundational PHP.

    ‍

    I’m self taught through a life in music, and I’m always inspired by the visual elements that accompany musical work. I also have a shaggy old sheepdog named Charlie. He’s a pretty good boy.
